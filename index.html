Features

Our well designed platform will not only cater the landlords convenience but also elevate the tenants experience.

Landlords

PUBLISH AND MANAGE PROPERTY

Show your property to interested tenants in a couple of clicks. We have made it super easy and elegant to share your listings on our platform.

share property listings

We will share your listing across the board with thousands of prospects to get you the best tenant.

know your tenants

Get a chance to know your tenants through credit scores and reviews from other landlords in order to choose the most appropriate tenant for your place.

Collect Rent Online

It's hard to keep a track of payments and remind your tenant to pay on time. We automate these payments so you don't have to - at the end of the month the money is in your bank account. Like magic!

DIGITIZE PAPERWORK

We all hate paperwork and we aim to remove it completely from the real estate world to make processes much simpler. All leases will be digital, allowing everyone to sign electronically and send it across or save a copy of it.

Next-generation system architecture

We will be rolling out updates to our platform allowing our customers to buy and sell real estate assets at a fraction of the cost by digitizing and automating intermediary processes. Stay tuned!

Tenants

find the Neighbourhood perfect to you

Tell us what you want in your neighbourhood. Nearby groceries? A workplace? Maybe a swimming pool for the summer? We'll help you find it! As simple as that.

Not just find, Explore places

Pick between an immersive virtual reality experience, or viewing the place first-hand. Should you choose the latter, we'll handle your appointments.

Connect with better roommates

Some like dogs, some like cats. We'll find you the purrrfect roommate, so it won't have to be so ruff.

Know your landlord

With a transparent review system, rest assured that the person you're dealing with lives up to their reputation.

Digitized paperwork

Reading all those lease documents can be a daunting task, even for the fastest readers. Ain't nobody got time for that! Good thing we give you the CliffNotes. Now you can claim to be the fastest reader out there.

Online rent payments

Tracking payments is a hassle. Why spend time making manual payments to your landlord when you could be watching Netflix? We got you. Choose how you'd like to pay, we sort out the rest!

Product Development

Effective June 9th, 2018
Phase One:
Research
Market Analysis

 In our initial research, we found out some big problems facing the real estate marketplace- rental scams, manual processes, too much paperwork and unpleasant experiences with landlords or tenants.

Proposed Solution

 Our platform is designed towards solving these issues and providing great customer experience by leveraging automated online payments, digitized paperwork and using algorithmically generated credit scores

Phase Two:
Development
Prototype Development

 With the help of our awesome customers, we were able to quickly test our initial hypotheses and iterate on our product to make it super useful to landlords and tenants. 

Iteratate for Improvement

As we learn more about problems in real estate industry, we will be rolling out more software updates to our platforms to help the needs of our customers. Please feel free to reach out for feature recommendations.

Phase Three:
Delivery
Deployment & Support

 Our teams are constantly working hard to incorporate features and test out our first version of the product. We expect to start deploying software in Q3 of 2018. Super excited!
